Hawthorne Lounge

Join us at the Hawthorne Theatre Lounge, the 21+ annex adjoining the Hawthorne Theatre. The Lounge has live music from local bands and national acts, tasty bar snacks, and a rotating menu of handcrafted cocktails. The Lounge is the perfect place on Hawthorne to grab a drink and relax while discovering your favorite new band.

Is your band interested in playing the Lounge? Check out our booking page for more information.
